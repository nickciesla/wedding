hotel details

room block at the Icona Windrift

We have a dedicated room block at the oceanfront Icona Windrift Hotel. Please note that there are two hotels called the Icona next door to one another. Our hotel and venue is the Icona Windrift, not the neighboring Icona Avalon.

The hotel amenities include a heated outdoor hot tub and pool where you can order lunch from the beach bar menu, a Tiki Bar overlooking the beach, five onsite dining options, direct beach access with beach chairs and umbrellas, complimentary beach cruiser bikes, a local shuttle around Avalon and Stone Harbor, and more. Parking at the hotel will be available for those using our room block (one vehicle per room).

105 80th St, Avalon, NJ 08202

what to do in avalon & stone harbor

Two great beach towns neighboring one another, Avalon and Stone Harbor are quintessential East Coast beach towns. If you are looking for ideas of what to do while in town, consider:

  • Cape May Beach Yoga
  • Hit the spa at The Reeds at Shelter Haven
  • Grab coffee at Coffe Talk, where Taylor Swift once performed as a kid
  • Pirate Island Mini Golf
  • Shop in downtown Stone Harbor
  • Grab ice cream at the famous Springer's Ice cream
  • Enjoy the Cape May Zoo
  • Visit Avalon Brew Pub at Icona Avalon next door
  • Golf at Stone Harbor Golf Club
  • Stop by Wildwood Board Walk
  • And, obviously, head to the beach
