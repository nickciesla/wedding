questions

Will the wedding be indoors or outdoors?

Weather permitting, the ceremony will be on the beach. You will enter the Icona Windrift Hotel and staff will direct you on how to access the beachfront ceremony. The reception will be indoors.

In the instance of inclement weather, the ceremony will be moved inside.

Can I bring a date or plus one?

We are so excited to celebrate the day with our closest friends and family. We are hoping to keep the guest list limited to those on your formal invitation (to be sent out closer to the wedding date). Thank you for understanding!

Can we bring our kids?

Our wedding is going to be adults-only (21+). We hope you enjoy the night off with us!

What if I miss the RSVP deadline?

If we do not receive your RSVP back by the deadline, we will mark you as not attending. We will miss you celebrating with us, however we have to provide our total guest count to our venue and cannot accept late RSVPs. Formal invitations with RSVP details will be sent out soon.

Will there be parking?

There will be free valet parking at the hotel. For those who are reserving rooms at The Icona Windrift Hotel, parking is included with your room (one car per room).

Will there be an after party?

You bet! We'll be at the same location throughout the night. For the after party, we'll head down stairs to Windrift bar called Level 1 to keep the party going. This portion of the evening will be a cash bar and you can order late night bites and personal pizzas.

What is the dress code?

The dress code is black tie at the beach. Formal attire is encouraged. Men should wear a suit jacket and tie. Women should consider full-length dresses or dressy separates. See inspiration below.
